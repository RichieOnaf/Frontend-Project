    The Six Fifteen Universe

    The Six Fifteen Universe is a connected world of Black heroes and heroines. A world with limitless potential told through the lens of
    colorful characters like: Evora Leigh (Evora Leigh: A New Breed), Amari King aka Maahes (The King's Domain), Malik Mitchell aka The
    Black Vigilante (The Legacy), and Hell Hawk (Hell Hawk). These series will eventually crossover but for now, they stand alone.
