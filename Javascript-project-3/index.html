Our History

Starbucks was founded in 1971 in Seattle, Washington. Our mission is to inspire and nurture the human spirit – one person, one cup, and one neighborhood at a time. We believe that every cup of coffee is an opportunity to make a positive impact in the world.

  • 1971

    Starbucks is founded by Jerry Baldwin, Zev Siegl, and Gordon Bowker in Seattle, Washington.

  • 1982

    Howard Schultz joins Starbucks as the director of operations and marketing.

  • 1987

    Starbucks opens its first location outside of Seattle in Chicago, Illinois.

  • 1992

    Starbucks goes public on the NASDAQ stock exchange.
